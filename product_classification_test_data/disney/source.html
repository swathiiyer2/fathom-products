so disappointing since you can rarely go wrong with donald & daisy
PostedJuly 14, 2017
aerith
 
 
Additional information about aerith could not be loaded.
this tsum's quality is upsetting considering how many amazing tsums donald has. he's not as cute in this one, i'm not sure what it is. the tsums also suffer from, as with other sets i've reviewed, some weird stitching that makes the faces kinda weird and a sloppy stitch work in the place that connect the face/neck to the body. the seam isn't nice and neat and tight and barely noticeable, it's jagged and loose and like someone just gave up on life.
the real disappointment was daisy though. her hair is a hot mess. it's not like how it looks in the production photo, it's closer to the fourth picture of them in a grassy area, but her hair isn't that full.
in fact, if you're unlucky like me, you're gonna get a daisy with thining "hair" and half of it shaved off with only a little bit attached to the hair near her neck. it was really, really disappointing. i had to waste money and make a whole new order since i'm going to try one more time for this set & hope it's of decent quality but i'm really put off by awful these newer tsums are.
